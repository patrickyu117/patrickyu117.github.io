About Me

Welcome to my About page! Here I will explain all about my love for video games.

One of my biggest passions are video games. I actually used be a very sporty kid growing up, however my parents decided to move which caused me to be further away from all of my friends. I used to be able to walk next door and ask my friends to play, but since I was too far away, I had to find another way to connect with people and pass the time. This is when I started to get more into video games. I found that multiplayer games were a great way to connect with other people, and do cool things that kids dream of like being a pirate, flying a spaceship, or being a superhero. My passion for video games continued when I built my first computer when I was 15. I was introduced to the world of PC gaming, and fell in love with competitive multiplayer games. This satisfied my competitive itch as I strived to climb through the ranks of games such as CS:GO and Call of Duty.
